OAKENBROOK TALES

~ Songs and Stories for Children ~

Welcome to Oakenbrook, home to Mother Mycelium, a tiny, wise mushroom matron who lives in a cozy hollow at the base of an old oak tree. With the help of the Forest Children (gentle caretakers of flowers, trees, dew, and other natural wonders) Mother Mycelium shares original songs and stories inspired by the rhythm of the seasons, the magic of nature, and the joy of learning something new.

You can explore Oakenbrook on YouTube and Bandcamp.

Updates

Created by a homeschooling, working mother, Oakenbrook grows as slowly and thoughtfully as the forest itself. It's a heart-led project that blends gentle fantasy, early childhood education, and lyrical storytelling. It’s designed for families seeking wholesome content that sparks wonder, nurtures curiosity, and feels like a soft mossy blanket on a quiet afternoon.

New content is shared Patreon and BuyMeACoffee... all free, no donation required.

I’d truly love to hear what you and your little ones think. Your feedback and kindness help me keep writing, recording, and growing this world, one story at a time.

Thanks so much for visiting. I'm so glad you're here.
