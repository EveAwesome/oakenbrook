Welcome to Oakenbrook Tales—a gentle, whimsical world of original songs and stories for children, inspired by the forest, the seasons, and the quiet magic of learning something new.

At the heart of Oakenbrook is Mother Mycelium, a tiny grandmotherly mushroom who shares stories with the Forest Children... and you!

Created by a homeschooling, working mother, Oakenbrook grows slowly like the forest itself. Everything here is free to enjoy. I would love to hear from you and what your little ones think!
